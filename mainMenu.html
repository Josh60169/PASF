PASF is an app to help you stay organized, keeping track of study times, and fined out what a grade on the final will get you.

On Multiple screens of this app you can scroll down like on the study timer window where you need to scroll down to see the arcade.

Click to open the Organizer and keep track of tasks

Click to open the Study Timer to time your work

Click to open the Final Calculator and calculate what you need on the final

Click to open the Account page and change account info or sign out
